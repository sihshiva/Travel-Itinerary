San Diego Itinerary

  • Explore Glasslamp Quarter and Bar Hopping (Save list of bars on Google maps) Ideas: lion Fish, Old Spaghetti Factory, Rustic Roof, The Field(Irish Pub), Ghirardelli
  • Night Club: OMNIA
